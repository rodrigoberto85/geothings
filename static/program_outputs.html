Spatial Analysis of Urban Area

Fealibility analysis for urban planner

Through a web interface, extract georeferenced information from urban areas, delivery data for urban planners.

  • Allows data extraction by a professional who does not specialize in spatial analysis
  • From an area defined in a map, generate preliminary data that support rapid decision making, without the need for field inspection.

For area of interest, the algoritm provive some kinds of output, that provide to reseacher some data that could be used in reseach
